The Perfect Steak

How to make a perfect Steak

The creme de la creme of proteins, the ribeye or new york strip steak yields a high fat content and tender texture

Ingredients

  1. 16 oz Ribeye or New York strip
  2. 1 clove Garlic
  3. 2 tbsp Butter
  4. 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  5. 1 sprig rosmemary
  6. 1 spring thymel
  7. Salt and Pepper to taste
  8. Onion and Garlic Powder to taste
