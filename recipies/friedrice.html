Fried Rice

Oriental Fried Rice

Fried Rice is a quick and easy dish to make using leftovers from your fridge and can easily be meal-prepped to fit a dynamic and fast-paced lifestyle

Ingredients

  1. 2 Cups Rice
  2. 1/2 cup any protein
  3. 1 Large Egg
  4. 2 Tbsp high smoke point oil (Canola or Vegetable)
  5. 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  6. 1 Tbsp Sesame oil
  7. 1 cup frozen Vegetable (carrots, corn, peas)
