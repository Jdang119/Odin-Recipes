Jinya Karaage Ramen

Jinya Ramen Bar - Spicy Crispy Chicken Ramen

This bowl of authentic Japanese ramen is comprised of an al-dente cooked wheat-flour based noodle, slow cooked spicy chicken broth, three pieces of crispy chicken karaage, and topped with seasoned egg, green onions, kikurage, and chili oil

Ingredients

  1. Create a rich broth from chicken or pork bones - cook for about 4 hours
  2. Buy store bought noodles because making your own noodles is a hassle
  3. Boil noodles until al-dente and cook protein bones and skim as needed
  4. Combine with crispy chicken, seasoned egg, green onion, kikurage, and chili oil
